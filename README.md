## 💬 Project Description

Despite the rise of modern messaging systems like Rocket.Chat, legacy email remains a dominant communication tool worldwide. This project aims to bridge the gap by creating a Rocket.Chat app that seamlessly integrates legacy email functionality using natural language commands, powered by Generative AI.

The app enables users to interact with their email accounts directly within Rocket.Chat, making email management more intuitive and collaborative.

### Key Features

-   **Summarize and Send**: Summarize a thread or discussion and send it as an email to specified recipients.
-   **Search and Share**: Search emails by date range and keywords, then share results or attachments directly in the channel.
-   **Daily Email Reports**: Generate and share daily email statistics.
-   **Secure Connections**: Ensure per-user email connections are secured over TLS.

### Example Commands

-   `/email-bridge summarize this thread and send it as email to my boss who refuses to use chat`
-   `/email-bridge post in the channel for everyone the budget for 2025 email PDF received between 11/1/2024 and 12/24/2024`
